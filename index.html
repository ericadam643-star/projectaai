How it works

From blank page
to final submission

Projecta gives you a structured path from your very first idea to your final submission. Every chapter has a clear workflow — from guided questions to supervisor review to approval.

01 ——
Sign up & set up
Create your account, enter your research topic, department, and connect to your supervisor using their unique code.
02 ——
Answer. Build. Write.
Answer focused questions that draw out your own thinking for each section. Your responses are structured into academic paragraphs — the ideas and arguments are entirely yours.
03 ——
Submit to supervisor
Your supervisor reviews with inline comments, highlight annotations, and structured feedback tools. You receive a clear action plan.
04 ——
Revise & get approved
Act on feedback, resubmit, and track your revision history. Every change is saved. Every approval is documented.
