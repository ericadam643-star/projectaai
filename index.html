Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

Built specifically for the Nigerian five-chapter final year project format.

✍️

Structured 5-chapter editor

Every chapter broken into the exact sections your university requires, with word count targets and writing guides.

🤖

Socratic AI guidance

The AI never writes for you — it asks questions that make you think, so your work stays authentically yours.

📚

Answer & write with citations

Answer structured questions and your responses become academic prose with proper in-text citations automatically.

💬

Supervisor annotation

Supervisors can highlight specific text and leave inline comments, just like marking a physical script.

🔍

Similarity check

Built-in detection flags suspicious phrases against online sources before submission.

📧

Email notifications

Students and supervisors get real email alerts on every submission and feedback event.

📱

Works on any device

Fully responsive — write from your phone, tablet, or laptop. Data syncs across all devices automatically.

⬇️

Download as Word or PDF

Export finished chapters in the format your department requires, ready for final submission.

🔒

Secure & private

Your project data is stored securely. Only you and your linked supervisor can see your work.
